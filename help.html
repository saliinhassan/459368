Sida Loo Isticmaalo Bogga

Bogga Fathia College waxa uu ka kooban yahay dhowr bog oo kala duwan:

  • Home: Waa bogga ugu horeeya. Waxaa ku yaal sawir soo dhaweyn ah iyo macluumaad kooban oo ku saabsan college-ka.
  • About Us: Halkaan waxaad ka akhrisan kartaa taariikhda college-ka, hadafkiisa, iyo adeegyada uu bixiyo.
  • Booking: Boggan waxaad ka helaysaa foom kuu oggolaanaya inaad codsato booqasho. Buuxi magacaaga, email, iyo fariin, kadibna riix badhanka "Dir".
  • Gallery: Halkaan waxaa lagu arki karaa sawirro kala duwan oo muujinaya college-ka, fasallada, i yo dhacdooyin.
  • Help: Boggan waxaad ka helaysaa tilmaamo ku saabsan sida loo isticmaalo website-ka.

Sida Loo isticmaalo Foomka (Form)

Si aad u codsato booqasho, ku buuxi foomka ku yaal bogga Booking. Gali magacaaga, email, dooro nooca booqashada, kadibna dir. Wixii qalad ah foomku wuu ku wargelin doonaa.

Sawirrada (Images)

Sawirrada bogga waxay muujinayaan deegaanka college-ka iyo ardayda wax baranaya. Marka la arko sawir, macnaha ama ujeeddada waxaa laga akhrisan karaa qoraalka ku xiga ama sareeya.
